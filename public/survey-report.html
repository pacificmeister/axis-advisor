Key Takeaways
Strategic insights from 68 real-world setups
  • 🚀
    The Surge Is the Breakout Product of 2025–2026 Surge dominates prone (#1 across all weights), is the fastest-growing choice in wing foiling, and is now penetrating tow-in, parawing, and SUP. Riders consistently report switching from Spitfire/ART and never looking back. The "game changer" phrase appears multiple times unprompted.
  • 🌊
    Tempo Owns Downwind — Fireball Is Universal Speed Tempo (960–1090) is the definitive DW choice for performance riders chasing speed. Fireball 1070 is the "Swiss Army knife" — appearing in Wing, DW, Parawing, Prone, and even Dock Start setups. The 1070 size specifically is the single most versatile wing in the range.
  • ⚙️
    Modularity Is AXIS's Most Valued Competitive Advantage Riders repeatedly cite the ability to swap just one component — front wing, fuselage, or rear wing — to transform a setup across disciplines. The Black Ultrashort + Surf Skinny 300 + 80cm UHM combination works from prone to wing to parawing. This cross-compatibility drives loyalty and upsell.
  • 📦
    Supply & Distribution Gaps Are Real Frustrations Multiple riders mention difficulty getting Surge wings (especially in Germany, South Africa). One rider specifically sold their Spitfire 1180 waiting for the Surge 1150. Demand clearly outpaces availability in certain markets — a distribution opportunity.
  • 🌍
    Global Reach — Local Gaps in Distribution 67 unique locations across 6 continents proves AXIS has true global reach — but feedback from South Africa, Ukraine, and inland Europe highlights unserved distribution territory. Switzerland alone has 5 respondents who are active community builders wanting official support.
  • 🎯
    Experienced Riders Drive Community Wisdom 88% have 2+ years of foiling experience, and 59% have 4+ years. This isn't a beginner survey — it's data from the community's most engaged and influential members. Their setups reflect genuine performance optimization, not marketing influence. These are the people who recommend gear to newcomers.
  • 📈
    Product Gaps The Community Is Asking For Surge 1150 (multiple requests), larger Tempos for heavier DW riders (>1090), bigger Surge sizes for lake/bay downwind runs, and a Surf Skinny 340 (specifically requested to pair with Surge 830). Riders are clearly signaling the next product tier they'll buy the moment it's available.
  • 💡
    The Advisor Tool Has Real-World Data to Build On With 68 setups mapped by weight, experience, and discipline, the AXIS Advisor now has a genuine recommendation engine grounded in community truth — not just catalog logic. The sweet spot: Surge 890 + Surf Skinny 300 + Black Ultrashort + 80cm UHM = the setup that covers prone, wing, parawing, and SUP for most 75–85kg riders.

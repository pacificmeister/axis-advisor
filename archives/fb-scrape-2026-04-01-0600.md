The kite is like a 3rd leg.... get rid of it and you suddenly notice how unstable foiling can be. Not sure your weight but 740 is a small foil to start on a foil drive for most riders, at 100 kg I am starting the 830 but need a decent push from chop or a wave. Back to the main point.... a larger stab will increase the pitch sensitivity but so will time. I would put in a couple more sessions and let your ears adjust... it's not an inherently unstable set up so it is just getting use to it. If you want an instant fix then longer fuse or bigger stab will help but at the cost of some of the roll and turn ability. Shimming the stab can also calm it down but can also make it hard to take off especially on the Foil Drive." (1 reaction)
